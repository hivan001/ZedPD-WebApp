Fort Hope

Three years have passed since the first confirmed case of the Z-Virus in humans. The infection spread rampant in large cities and eventually devastated even the smallest of towns. In the subsequent years, the worldwide human population has decreased by 85% leaving the remaining survivors desperate to return to a sense of normalcy. Despite all of the hardships of the post-apocolyptic world, small communities have started coming together to increase their chances of survival against depletion of resources and the undead.

Fort Hope is a small community of 5,000 residents in rural Montana. Citizens of Fort Hope each contribute to the community with their own set of skills such as farming, carpentry and security. Your role in Fort Hope is that of the local sheriff whose goal is to keep the town safe from the undead and keeping the peace at home. You are also tasked with making supply runs into the nearby city of Billings to get those much needed supplies.
